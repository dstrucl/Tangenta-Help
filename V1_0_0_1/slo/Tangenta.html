TANGENTA-INVOICING
Tangenta is free open-source database program for selling
goods and services in small or medium businesses.
You can write invoices and proforma-invoices and copy data between them.
Tangenta has it's unique user interface where you always see how tables and columns are related to each other.
Connection to SQLite or MSSQL database. SQLite is very convenient for one or two persons who are working on the same machine. For multiuser operation select MSSQL server.
Support for 3 different concurrent modes of selling:
Shop A: Simple without pricelist
Shop B: integer quantities of items from pricelist and no stock
Shop C: decimal quantities with physical units of items from pricelist,
stock or directly from factory
